Health Guide: Setup and Prevention of Common Illnesses

1. Dengue Fever
Symptoms: High fever, headache, rash, muscle and joint pain.
Prevention: Eliminate standing water, use mosquito repellent, wear long sleeves.
Treatment: Rest, hydration, pain relievers (Paracetamol). Avoid Aspirin/Ibuprofen.

2. Influenza (Flu)
Symptoms: Fever, cough, sore throat, runny nose, body aches.
Prevention: Annual vaccination, hand washing, avoiding close contact with sick people.
Treatment: Rest, fluids, antivirals if prescribed early.

3. Headache
Types: Tension, Migraine, Cluster.
Remedies: Hydration, rest in dark room, OTC pain relievers, stress management.
Warning: Seek medical help if headache is sudden and severe (thunderclap).
